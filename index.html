Rylee Shaye Music

Rylee Shaye is a Huntsville-based singer, songwriter, and performer who blends nostalgic pop energy with a modern, colorful edge. Known for her vibrant stage presence and signature purple guitar, Rylee transforms every show into a feel-good time capsule that spans decades of sound from early 2000s pop to 90s grunge and vintage country.

A lifelong performer, Rylee’s path runs from early sets at the Spokane County State Fair to reaching 18,000 live viewers on Reddit’s now-retired public broadcast network, and on to steady content creation across YouTube and TikTok. Lately in North Alabama, she’s a regular at Bridge Street Town Centre and recently took part in Make Music Day Huntsville.

Her sets are equal parts charisma and catharsis, pulling listeners in with expressive vocals and playful storytelling. Whether reimagining classics like “Bring Me to Life” and “Genie in a Bottle” or performing original songs ranging from acoustic renditions of 80's synth style songs about driving in Miami, or deeply personal songs about overcoming mental health struggles, Rylee delivers authenticity that resonates.
